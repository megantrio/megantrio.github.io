About Me

From a young age, I pursued a deep passion for music, performing in various settings such as a youth orchestra and church band. I dedicated significant time to mastering the piano, earning admission to a jazz music school. I utilize my keen listening abilities and various sensory tools to enrich my auditory experience, constantly striving to help others discover better ways to appreciate sound.

Outside of work, I enjoy reading and exploring computer technology in peaceful environments. My aim is to create experiences that help people listen more attentively.
